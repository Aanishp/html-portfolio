The Best Movies According to Aanish

My Top 5 Movies of ALL-Time.

1. Toy Story 4 (2019)

When a new toy called "Forky" joins Woody and the gang, a road trip alongside old and new friends reveals how big the world can be for a toy.

2. Detective Byomkesh Bakshy! (2015)

Detective Byomkesh Bakshy is set in the 2nd World War torn Calcutta during the 1940s. Enter a magical world of nostalgia and 'Expect the Unexpected'.

3. Avengers: Endgame (2019)

After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War (2018), the universe is in ruins. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe.

4. Kantara (2022)

When greed paves the way for betrayal, scheming and murder, a young tribal reluctantly dons the traditions of his ancestors to seek justice.

5. Andhadhun (2018)

A series of mysterious events change the life of a blind pianist, who must now report a crime that he should technically know nothing of.
